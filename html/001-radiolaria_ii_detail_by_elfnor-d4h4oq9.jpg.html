Radiolaria II - detail

Dimensions: 400 mm diameter
Materials: Ceramic, glaze, copper wire
2011

Close up detail showing the construction and internal frame. This was made as separate modules, (cones and rings) and assembled after firing. There is an internal frame soldered together from heavy copper wire to keep everything in place.
