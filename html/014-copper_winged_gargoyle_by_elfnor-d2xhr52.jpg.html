Copper Winged Gargoyle

Dimensions: 200 x 200 x 150 mm
Materials: Ceramic, glaze, copper, bone
2002

This gargoyle is designed to perch on the edge of a shelf, looking down. He looks great sitting on the corner of a computer monitor watching what you're doing.

The wings are bone spikes with copper plates. The body is ceramic with a copper barium glaze.
