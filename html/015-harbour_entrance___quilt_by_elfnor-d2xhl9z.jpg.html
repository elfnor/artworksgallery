Harbour Entrance - Quilt

Dimensions: 620 x 450 mm
Materials: Cotton Fabric
1998

This landscape quilt of the entrance to Wellington Harbour at sunset, uses techniques from Joen Wolfrom's book Landscapes and Illusions. All the fabrics are hand dyed. Machine pierced and quilted.
