Young Girl Reading

Dimensions: 100 x 100 x 100 mm
Materials: Ceramic with oxide wash
2010

This is the one of number of small seated figurines I've made. It is based on a photo from National geographic. I'm fascinated by the number of ways people sit on the ground, especially when they're working or concentrating on something. People use their feet and knees to brace their arms and hands in some great natural poses. I'm also absorbed by how cloth drapes over the figure when sitting like this.
