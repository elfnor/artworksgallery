Still Life Quilt

Dimensions: 750 x 650 mm
Material: Cotton Fabric
2000

This quilt was made for a challenge competition. The challenge was to make a non rectangular quilt under one metre square incorporating the mystery fabric.

The challenge fabric was a large scale oriental style print with cranes and chrysanthemums. I've used in on the main vase and on the pattern bands on the bowls and plate. I combined it with hand dyed fabrics. The shadows were done by over painting the printed fabric with washes of fabric paint.
