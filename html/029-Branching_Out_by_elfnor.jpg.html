Branching Out

Dimensions: 400 mm x 400 mm
Materials: Ceramic, glaze, wire
2008

This tensegrity sculpture has the same underlying structure as "The Other Way Round". It consists of six ceramic pieces suspended from each other by fine wire. The pieces do not touch each other. The finish is a raku crackle glaze.
